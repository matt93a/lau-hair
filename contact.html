Our Location

The Lau Hairdressing Salon can be found at the centre of Maidstone, Kent, known as the Garden of England. Located at 13 Gabriel's Hill, only a five minute walk from the Maidstone East train station. The Mall shopping centre car park is also only five minutes away and has 1000 car parking spaces. Two minutes from the salon is the town centre Black Cab Taxi rank to all destinations.
