## Buttons

- **Primary:** Solid black background (`#000`), white text, no border radius (or very small ~2px), padding ~14px 28px
- **Secondary:** White/transparent background, thin black border (1px), black text
- Font: Montserrat, regular weight

## Header

- Background: black (`#000`)
- Logo: white "Lau" wordmark, centred
- Left: hamburger / menu icon (3 vertical dots on current site)
- Right: social icons (X, Facebook, Instagram), phone number `01622 670 557`, "Book now" button
- Height: ~80px desktop
- Above the header: gold notification strip (`#B09F65`) with "WE'RE HIRING" / news messaging

## Aesthetic summary

Clean, premium, monochromatic. White space heavy. Thin/light typography. Restrained gold accent. Photography-led — large model imagery paired with dark quote panels. Hand-drawn / artistic logo treatment that contrasts with the otherwise clean modernist layout.
