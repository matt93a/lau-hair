From the chair

3 at-home habits that keep colour fresh for longer
01 · Cooler washes. Hot water opens the cuticle and lets colour bleed out. Rinse as cool as you can stand, especially in the first 72 hours after a colour service.
02 · Sulphate-free shampoo. Harsh surfactants are the single biggest cause of colour fade at home. Ask us in-salon which product matches your hair — we'll get it right for your tone.
03 · A weekly bond treatment. Olaplex or K18 once a week keeps hair stronger and shinier — and genuinely helps colour last longer by sealing the cuticle.
